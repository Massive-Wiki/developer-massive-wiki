<h1>Diplomatic and Normalized Transcriptions</h1> <p><em>First version created by copying in from Pete's local personal wiki, 2024-01-31</em></p> <p>In handwriting transcription, we have "diplomatic" transcription and "normalized" transcription. (And semi-diplomatic and semi-normalized....)</p> <p>"Diplomatic" transcription tries to copy the text exactly, with archaic orthography and what-have-you.</p> <p>"Normalized" transcription tries to make the text readable by a modern reader.</p> <p>Resources:</p> <ul> <li><a href="https://tei-c.org/Vault/ETE/Preview/driscoll.html">TEI: Levels of Transcription (Driscoll)</a></li> </ul>
